(Midterm) Non-Digital Game: I Have It
I Have It is a poker game that players need to play poker to battle in each turn and the meanwhile they need to collect target cards or run out of others’ cards. In each turn, players need to play cards based on number and suits so that they can draw cards. If they do not have eligible cards to play, they need to let the previous player draw cards from their hands. The player who collects the target combination of cards or runs out of others’ cards first wins.
